# Domain Strategies for Celebioglu Businesses

## 1. Research Process
- Use ExpiredDomains.net daily
- Filters: .com priority, keywords (alu, metal, marine, crew, ticket, trade, billet, scrap)
- Metrics: High ABY, BL > 100, clean history

## 2. Acquisition
- Dropped domains: Immediate reg via Namecheap/GoDaddy
- Auctions & Backorders
- Budget: Start small ($200-500/month)

## 3. Usage
- Brand new sites for MEASVIA and Aluminum divisions
- 301 redirects for SEO
- Flipping on Sedo/Afternic

## 4. Risks & Best Practices
- Check spam/penalties
- Trademark clearance
- Regular renewal
